One common approach is to perform collision detection based on the object's position in the game world
rather than relying solely on the drawing order

Giải quyết cho vấn đề hồi nãy:
By performing collision detection based on the object's position and bounding boxes, 
we can accurately detect collisions regardless of the drawing order. 
This ensures that collisions are properly detected even if one object is drawn on top of another.
